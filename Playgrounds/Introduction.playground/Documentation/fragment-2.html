Operators & Functions

ChannelZ's central operator is ∞, which can be entered with Option-5 on the Mac keyboard. Variants of this operator are used throughout the framework, but you can alternatively use functions for all of ChannelZ's operations. The major operators are listed in section Operator Glossary.
